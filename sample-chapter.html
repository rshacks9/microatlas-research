Accounts Payable Automation for Construction Subcontractors

Executive summary

Construction subcontractors live in a paperwork trap: paper invoices, lien-waiver chase, job-cost coding errors, and payment timing that determines whether a job is profitable. AP automation built for generalist finance teams usually fails here because it does not understand the lien-waiver workflow, the project-based cost code, or the relationship between the field and the back office.

The opportunity is a vertical AP product—or wrapper—that turns invoice-to-payment into a compliance-correct, margin-visible workflow for specialty trades.

Buyer jobs-to-be-done

  • Capture invoices without manual data entry.
  • Match invoices to purchase orders and job codes.
  • Collect and track lien waivers before releasing payment.
  • Accelerate approval routing across project managers and owners.
  • Optimize payment timing to capture early-pay discounts and preserve cash.

Market signal

Search intent around "construction AP automation," "subcontractor invoice software," and "lien waiver automation" is rising. The horizontal AP automation market is growing at roughly 20% annually, but construction-specific attach rates remain low, indicating an underserved vertical.

Pricing and packaging

Successful vendors price by monthly platform fee ($500–$5,000 by revenue band), per-invoice fee, or embedded payments take-rate. Implementation fees are common because workflow design and ERP integration are project-like.
