Checkout setup

PayPal checkout is almost live.

This page appears when a buy button does not yet have a PayPal URL. Add your PayPal business identifier, run scripts/apply_checkout_links.py, then redeploy.

Activate checkout

  1. Confirm your PayPal Business account can receive payments and copy the business email or Merchant ID.
  2. Set the environment variable: export PAYPAL_BUSINESS_ID="your-id"
  3. Set your site URL: export MICROATLAS_SITE_URL="https://rshacks9.github.io/microatlas-research"
  4. Run python3 scripts/apply_checkout_links.py.
  5. Redeploy public_paid_site/.

Prefer Payment Links? Create each product manually in PayPal and paste the URL into the checkout_url column of checkout_links.csv. Those URLs override the generated links.
