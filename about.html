About

Deep maps of overlooked markets.

What we do

MicroAtlas Research publishes first-edition, 120-page research reports on niche industries where search demand outruns credible, purchase-ready analysis. Each report combines public-source signals, buyer-intent research, value-chain analysis, pricing logic, unit economics, GTM playbooks, compliance and risk notes, and a diligence appendix.

Who we are

MicroAtlas Research is run by Tom Gaffney, an independent researcher and former operating advisor to vertical SaaS, services, and consumer-technology companies. He is not a physician, clinician, or healthcare provider, and MicroAtlas reports are business-strategy research, not medical, legal, investment, or regulated professional advice.

First-edition discipline

We ship fast, mark assumptions plainly, and treat long-tail markets with boardroom rigor. Readers should validate exact market-size, compliance, and investment claims with primary research and counsel before acting.
